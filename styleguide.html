Utility

Utility Principali

Il framework include un set completo di utility responsive (es. p-md, md:p-lg, w-full, lg:w-1/2, flex, sm:flex-col, shadow-lg, rounded-xl, ecc.)

Esempio Sizing & Spacing:

Card A

.w-full .md:w-1/2 .p-lg .rounded-lg .shadow-md

Card B

.w-full .md:w-1/2 .p-lg .rounded-lg .shadow-md .mt-md .md:mt-0

<div class="md:flex gap-md">
    <div class="w-full md:w-1/2 p-lg rounded-lg shadow-md">
        ...
    </div>
    <div class="w-full md:w-1/2 p-lg rounded-lg shadow-md mt-md md:mt-0">
        ...
    </div>
</div>
